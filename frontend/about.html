About This Project

I built this job portal as part of my internship with IntrnForte. It took me a few weeks to complete. I learned a lot about backend development and database design. The site lets employers post jobs and job seekers apply. It was a great learning experience.

Welcome to JobPortal!.

Our mission is to connect job seekers with their dream jobs and help employers find the best talent. We believe in creating a platform that is user-friendly, efficient, and inclusive for everyone.
